Discover Our Fragrance Collection

1. Enchanted Blossom

Enchanted Blossom is a delicate floral fragrance that captures the essence of a sunlit garden in spring. Top notes of fresh bergamot and juicy pear intertwine with a heart of blooming jasmine and peony, creating a vibrant bouquet that evokes feelings of joy and renewal. The base notes of warm sandalwood and soft musk linger gently on the skin, adding depth and sophistication. Perfect for daytime wear, this perfume is a celebration of femininity and grace, ideal for those who wish to leave a trail of enchantment wherever they go.

2. Midnight Oud

For those who embrace the allure of the night, Midnight Oud is an opulent and mysterious fragrance that unfolds like a rich tapestry. With top notes of smoky incense and spicy saffron, it opens with a bold statement that commands attention. The heart reveals luxurious notes of rose and amber, intertwining to create a warm, inviting embrace. The foundation of this scent is a deep, lingering oud and creamy vanilla, which adds a sensual complexity that lasts all night long. This fragrance is perfect for evening wear and special occasions, exuding confidence and sophistication.

3. Oceanic Breeze

Oceanic Breeze is a refreshing and invigorating scent that transports you to sun-kissed shores and crystal-clear waters. This fragrance opens with bright citrus notes of lemon and lime, immediately awakening the senses. The heart features a blend of sea salt and green apple, creating a crisp and clean aroma reminiscent of a gentle ocean breeze. As it settles, base notes of driftwood and white musk ground the scent, offering a subtle warmth. Ideal for everyday wear, this perfume embodies the spirit of adventure and freedom, perfect for those who love to explore.
